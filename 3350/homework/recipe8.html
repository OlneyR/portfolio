Spicy Shrimp Pasta
Ingredients
2T butter 0.5 red and green bell peppers, diced
4 minced cloves of garlic 0.5C diced green onions
1.5T creole or cajun seasoning 1lb shrimp
0.5C whipping cream around 0.5C red wine
8oz cooked linguine 1/3C grated parmesan
2t red pepper flakes 1/3C chopped parsley
Directions:

Melt butter in skillet on high, add the red and green bell peppers, the garlic, the green onions, and the creole seasoning. Cook for 1 minute.

Add the shrimp and the whipping cream, reduce heat to medium and simmer 3 minutes while stirring.

Add wine and simmer another 3 minutes.

Finally, add the linguine, parmesan, red pepper flakes and parsley.
